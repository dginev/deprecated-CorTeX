Let $M$ be a module.  A {\it flat resolution} of $M$
is an exact sequence of the form
$$\cdots \to F_n \to F_{n-1} \to \cdots \to F_1 \to F_0 \to M \to 0$$
where each $F_n$ is a flat module.
