Let $G$ be a group, $X$ a set, and $\cdot: G \times X \longrightarrow X$ a group action. For any $x \in X$, the {\em orbit} of $x$ under the group action is the set
$$
\{g\cdot x \mid g \in G\} \subset X.
$$
