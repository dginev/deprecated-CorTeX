Let $ABCD$ be a parallelogram with side lengths $u,v$ and whose diagonals have lengths $d_1$ and $d_2$ then
$$2u^2+2v^2=d_1^2 + d_2^2.$$


\begin{center}
\framebox{
\begin{pspicture*}(-1,-1)(6,3)
\pspolygon(0,0)(4,0)(5,2)(1,2)
\uput[270](2,-0.3){$u$}
\uput[180](0.3,1){$v$}
\qline(0,0)(5,2)
\qline(4,0)(1,2)
\uput[0](1.7,0.5){$d_1$}
\uput[0](1.8,1.5){$d_2$}
\end{pspicture*}
}
