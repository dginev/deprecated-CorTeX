Let $x$ and $r$ be real numbers.
If\; $0>r>-1$ or $r>1$ and $x>-1$ then
$$(1+x)^r\ge 1+xr.$$
\smallskip

The inequality also holds when $r$ is an even integer.
For $0<r<1$ the inverse inequality holds.
