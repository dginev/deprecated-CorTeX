Given an integer $n > 0$,

$$M(n) = \sum_{i = 1}^n \mu(i)$$

where $\mu(x)$ is the M\"obius function.

There is no $x$ such that $M(x) > x$. Franz Mertens conjectured that there is no $x$ such that $M(x) > \sqrt{x}$, but this was proven false later.
