A {\it (left) skew derivation}
on a ring $R$ is a pair $(\sigma, \delta)$,
where $\sigma$ is a ring endomorphism of $R$,
and $\delta$ is a left \PMlinkname{$\sigma$-derivation}{SigmaDerivation} on $R$.
